Final Project

Your final project will be the sole focus of the remainder of the semester. The remaining classes will be lab time for this project and I will be available to help. I will not be as hands-on as with the mid-term projects, this is your chance to show and hone your skills.

The project is an individual project, it is due December 17th at 5pm.

I will be grading based on creativity and use of skills learned in class.

Creativity does not mean pretty, you can be creative with your code solutions.

Your project must incorporate at least 6 points based on the following skills:

  • Mouse/Key Input
  • Graphics
  • Objects
  • Lists
  • Images
  • Sound
  • Networked/Connected programs
    • The server still running for your use: romeo.crhallberg.com:8120
  • Video
  • Libraries
  • Text input and manipulation
  • File analysis
  • Website Integration

Use of a skill will get you 1 point. Advanced use of a skill will get you 2 points. In order for a skill to qualify as advanced, you must use the skill outside of the scope taught in class (demonstrating research), use in a way not directly covered in class (color analysis of a picture), or thoroughly explore a skill as a core factor of your project. I will clarify upon request and feel free to clarify intention to use of a skill in an advanced way when you make your submission.
